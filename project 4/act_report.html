Data Wrangling - We Rate Dogs (Twitter)¶

Introduction¶

The dataset for this project is the twitter archieve of a twitter user @dog_rates also known as WeRateDogs. The twitter user rates people dog with humorous comments and has gained massive popularity and recognition online. The data was gathered from three sources; the twitter-archive-enhanced.csv, image_prediction.tsv and tweet-json.txt as described in the wrangle_report.The dataset contains 2356 tweets of the 5000 between November 2015 and August 2017.

Data Wrangling¶

Data wrangling activities were conducted including gathering the data (Both manual and programatic downloading of data file from Udacity sever), accessing the data(Both visually and programmatically) and cleaning the data. The wrangle_report file is a separate report containing details of these steps.

Insights Derived¶

  1. Most dog were rated between 9 - 13.
  2. iPhone is the major source of the pictures
  3. Favorite count has a strong positive linear relationship with retweet count
  4. Peak retweet engagement with WeRateDogs for the period of analysis was in June 2016
  5. There were so much Retriever with Golden retriever being the most predicted breed and Labrador retriever being next to it.
  6. People loved the Saluki breed
